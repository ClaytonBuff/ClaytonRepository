About Sam Givhan

  • Businessman, Attorney and 7th Generation Alabama Farmer
  • Helped to bring countless jobs to Huntsville and Madison County
  • Bachelors in Finance from the University of Auburn
  • J.D. from the University of Alabama School of Law
  • Senior Vice-Chairman, Alabama Republican Party (2012-2018)
  • Chairman, Madison County Republican Party (2012-2018)
  • Real Estate Attorney, Wilmer & Lee, P.A.
  • Board Member, Huntsville Madison County Builders Association
  • Owner, Houston Land Company and SHG Development
  • Member, Huntsville’s Economic Development Team
  • President of Habitat for Humanity of Madison County (2012-2018)
  • Member, Huntsville Rotary Club

Sam is married to Heather King Givhan, and the father of six children: Jacob, Anderson, Sarah Beth, Mary Katherine, Houston, and Bennett. The Givhans are members of Whitesburg Baptist Church.
